I am a fashion/textiles designer and a Central Saint Martins Fashion Print student, currently based in London. With my work I’m trying to create my own universe, where vivid colors, love for human form and geometry, elaborate hand work and positivity come together in kaleidoscopic compositions that are fun, playful and which hopefully can bring joy to others. Whether it’s my interest in countercultures of the past and present, love for primitive art or my traditional Russian roots – handcraft, obsession with detail and intricate surface enhancement through print, texture and embroidery have become key to my practice. I am interested in constantly expanding my mind and practice with new ideas and challenges, as well as with enriching creative collaborations.
